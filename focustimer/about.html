About Focus Timer

Focus Timer is a simple and effective Pomodoro technique timer app designed to help you boost productivity and maintain focus during work sessions.

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. It uses a timer to break work into focused intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. This technique helps improve focus, reduce mental fatigue, and increase productivity.

How Focus Timer Works

Focus Timer implements the Pomodoro Technique with a modern, user-friendly interface:

  • Work Sessions: 25-minute focused work periods (customizable)
  • Short Breaks: 5-minute breaks between work sessions (customizable)
  • Long Breaks: 15-minute breaks after 4 work sessions (customizable)
  • Auto-Start: Automatically begin the next session to maintain flow
