Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

Welcome to Focus Timer! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Pomodoro timer application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Task Names: When you add task names to your work sessions, this information is stored locally on your device.
  • App Settings: Your timer preferences, including work duration, break times, and notification settings are stored locally.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Statistics: The app tracks your completed sessions, work time, and productivity patterns locally on your device.
  • Device Information: Basic device information (Android version, app version) for app functionality and updates.

1.3 AdMob Integration

  • Advertisements: Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data according to their own privacy policy.
  • Ad Identifiers: AdMob may use advertising identifiers to provide personalized ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 App Functionality

  • To provide and maintain the Focus Timer service
  • To track your productivity statistics and session history
  • To customize your timer settings and preferences
  • To send notifications when timer sessions end

2.2 Analytics and Improvements

  • To analyze app usage patterns and improve functionality
  • To fix bugs and enhance user experience
  • To develop new features based on user needs

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your data, including task names, settings, and statistics, is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.

3.2 Data Security

  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
  • Your data is stored securely on your device
  • We do not transmit sensitive personal information to external servers

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob

Our app integrates with Google AdMob for advertising purposes. AdMob's collection and use of data is governed by their own privacy policy, which you can review at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

4.2 Google Play Services

We use Google Play Services for app functionality and updates. Google's privacy policy applies to their services.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Data Access and Control

  • You can view and modify your app settings at any time
  • You can clear your task history and statistics through the app settings
  • You can uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data

5.2 Advertising Preferences

  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings
  • You can reset your advertising identifier in your device settings

6. Children's Privacy

Focus Timer is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Data Retention

  • Your app data is stored locally on your device
  • Data is retained until you clear it through app settings or uninstall the app
  • We do not maintain copies of your personal data on our servers

8. International Data Transfers

Since your data is stored locally on your device, no international data transfers occur. However, third-party services like AdMob may transfer data according to their own policies.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for processing your personal information includes:

  • Consent: You have given clear consent for us to process your personal data for specific purposes
  • Legitimate Interest: Processing is necessary for our legitimate interests in providing and improving our app
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to provide you with the app services

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected and how it's used
  • Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale of personal information
  • Access your personal information
  • Equal service and price (non-discrimination)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided above.
