About SpendTrack

Revolutionizing personal finance management with privacy-first technology.

Built by BashTech for smart financial tracking

Our Mission

To empower individuals with smart, privacy-focused financial management tools that work seamlessly with their existing banking infrastructure.

We believe that managing your finances should be effortless, secure, and completely private. SpendTrack bridges the gap between traditional banking and modern financial management, all while keeping your data exactly where it belongs - on your device.

What Makes SpendTrack Special

Privacy-First Design

Your financial data never leaves your device. All processing happens locally, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Offline Functionality

Works without internet connection. Your expense tracking continues even when offline, with full synchronization when connected.

Smart Automation

Automatically detects and categorizes transactions from bank SMS messages, saving you time and effort.

Insightful Analytics

Get detailed spending insights, trends, and reports to make better financial decisions.

Our Story

2023 - The Beginning

SpendTrack was born from the frustration of manually tracking expenses and concerns about privacy in financial apps.

2024 - Development & Testing

Months of development focused on creating a privacy-first solution that works seamlessly with existing banking infrastructure.

2024 - Launch

SpendTrack launches on Google Play Store, bringing secure and automated expense tracking to users worldwide.
