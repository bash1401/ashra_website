Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Learn how we protect your financial data.

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to SpendTrack, developed by BashTech. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using SpendTrack, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

SpendTrack is designed with privacy-first principles. Here's what we collect and how:

SMS Messages

We read SMS messages from your selected banks to automatically detect and categorize financial transactions. This is the core functionality of our app.

Device Information

Basic device information such as device model, operating system version, and app version for technical support and app optimization.

Important: All transaction data is processed and stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your financial data to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Automatically detect and categorize financial transactions from bank SMS messages
  • Provide expense tracking and financial insights
  • Generate spending reports and analytics
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance

4. Data Security

We implement several security measures to protect your data:

Local Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using encrypted local database storage.

No Cloud Storage

We do not store your financial data on external servers or cloud platforms.

Encryption

All sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption algorithms.

Access Control

Only you have access to your data through the app's secure authentication.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. However, we may disclose your information in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or to comply with legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With your explicit consent
Your Control: You have complete control over your data. You can delete all app data at any time through your device settings.

6. Cookies and Tracking

SpendTrack does not use cookies or tracking technologies. Since all data is processed locally on your device, we do not need to track your online activity.

7. Children's Privacy

SpendTrack is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, no international data transfers occur. Your data remains within your device's jurisdiction.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
