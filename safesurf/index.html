About SafeSurf

SafeSurf is a lightweight privacy app designed to protect your DNS queries using DNS over HTTPS (DoH). Unlike traditional VPNs, SafeSurf focuses specifically on DNS privacy - it's faster and uses less battery because it only secures DNS traffic.

Our app helps prevent passive observers from seeing your domain lookups while maintaining excellent performance. Whether you're concerned about privacy, want to bypass DNS-level blocking, or simply prefer encrypted DNS, SafeSurf provides a simple and effective solution.

Note: SafeSurf protects DNS lookup privacy only. It does not route or encrypt all application traffic like a full VPN.
