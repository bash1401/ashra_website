Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Learn how SafeSurf protects your DNS privacy.

Last updated: August 16, 2025

About SafeSurf

SafeSurf is a DNS-over-HTTPS (DoH) VPN application developed by BashTech that protects your DNS privacy by routing DNS queries through secure, encrypted servers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. SafeSurf does NOT collect, log, or store:

  • DNS queries - We do not log what websites you visit or DNS requests you make
  • Browsing history - We cannot see or record your internet activity
  • IP addresses for tracking purposes - Your real IP address is not logged by our service
  • Network traffic contents - We only handle DNS requests, not web traffic
  • Personal identification information - Names, emails, or other personal data (unless you contact us)

2. Information We May Collect

To provide and improve our service, we may collect limited information:

Device and App Information
  • Device identifiers (including advertising ID)
  • App performance data and crash reports
  • Usage statistics and performance metrics
  • Device type and operating system
Purchase Information
  • Subscription status
  • Purchase verification through Google Play
  • App settings and preferences
  • Consent choices for GDPR compliance

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide VPN service through secure DoH servers (Cloudflare 1.1.1.1 and Google 8.8.8.8)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Manage subscriptions and process premium purchases
  • Serve relevant advertisements (if not premium subscriber)
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements

4. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

We use third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

Purpose: Display advertisements in the free version

Data: Advertising ID, device information, usage patterns

Purpose: Process premium subscription purchases

Data: Purchase transaction details and subscription status

Policy: Cloudflare Privacy Policy

Data: DNS queries (not logged by SafeSurf)

Policy: Google Privacy Policy

5. Data Security and Protection

We implement several security measures to protect your data:

DNS Encryption

All DNS queries are encrypted using DNS-over-HTTPS (DoH) protocol.

No Logging

We do not log, store, or track your DNS queries or browsing activity.

Secure Servers

We use trusted DNS providers with strong privacy policies.

Access Control

Limited access to collected data with strict internal controls.

6. International Data Transfers

SafeSurf operates globally and may transfer data internationally:

  • DNS queries are routed to servers in various countries for optimal performance
  • We use cloud services that may store data in different jurisdictions
  • All transfers comply with applicable data protection laws
  • We implement appropriate safeguards for international data transfers

7. Children's Privacy

SafeSurf is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date on this page and posting a notice in the app. Your continued use of SafeSurf after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
