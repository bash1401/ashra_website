SafeSurf is a DNS-over-HTTPS (DoH) VPN application developed by BashTech that protects your DNS privacy by routing DNS queries through secure, encrypted servers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. SafeSurf does NOT collect, log, or store:

  • DNS queries - We do not log what websites you visit or DNS requests you make
  • Browsing history - We cannot see or record your internet activity
  • IP addresses for tracking purposes - Your real IP address is not logged by our service
  • Network traffic contents - We only handle DNS requests, not web traffic
  • Personal identification information - Names, emails, or other personal data (unless you contact us)

2. Information We May Collect

To provide and improve our service, we may collect limited information:

2.1 Device and App Information

  • Device identifiers - Including advertising ID for personalized ads (if not opted out)
  • App performance data - Crash reports, error logs, and diagnostic information
  • Usage statistics - Anonymous data about app features used and performance metrics
  • Device type and operating system - For compatibility and optimization purposes

2.2 Purchase Information

  • Subscription status - Whether you have an active premium subscription
  • Purchase verification - Transaction details processed through Google Play Billing

2.3 Preference Data

  • App settings - Your VPN preferences and configuration choices
  • Consent choices - Your advertising and analytics consent decisions (for GDPR compliance)

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide VPN service - Route DNS queries through secure DoH servers (Cloudflare 1.1.1.1 and Google 8.8.8.8)
  • Improve app performance - Fix bugs, optimize features, and enhance user experience
  • Manage subscriptions - Process premium purchases and verify subscription status
  • Serve relevant ads - Display personalized advertisements (if not premium subscriber)
  • Ensure security - Detect and prevent abuse or security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations - Meet regulatory requirements and respond to legal requests

4. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

We use third-party services that may collect information:

4.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

  • Purpose: Display advertisements in the free version of our app
  • Data collected: Advertising ID, device information, app usage patterns
  • Data sharing: Anonymous advertising data shared with Google and advertising partners
  • Control: You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings or upgrade to Premium
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

4.2 Google Play Billing (Subscriptions)

  • Purpose: Process premium subscription purchases
  • Data collected: Purchase transaction details and subscription status
  • Data sharing: Transaction data processed through Google Play Store

4.3 DNS Service Providers

  • Cloudflare (1.1.1.1): Primary DNS-over-HTTPS provider
  • Google DNS (8.8.8.8): Secondary DNS-over-HTTPS provider
  • Data sharing: Your DNS queries are sent to these providers but are not logged by SafeSurf
  • Policies: Cloudflare Privacy Policy | Google DNS Privacy

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local storage: App preferences and premium status stored locally on your device
  • Encryption: All DNS queries are encrypted using HTTPS (DoH protocol)
  • Security measures: Industry-standard security practices to protect data
  • Data retention: Diagnostic data retained for up to 90 days, then automatically deleted

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 For European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification - Correct any inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure - Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability - Receive your data in a structured format
  • Object - Object to processing of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent - Withdraw consent for advertising at any time

6.2 Advertising Controls

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Android Settings > Privacy > Ads > Delete advertising ID
  • Premium subscription: Remove all ads by upgrading to Premium
  • GDPR consent: Manage your advertising consent through our in-app consent dialog

7. Children's Privacy
