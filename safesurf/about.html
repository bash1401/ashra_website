About SafeSurf

By BashTech

SafeSurf is a lightweight privacy app that protects your DNS queries using DNS over HTTPS (DoH). It encrypts DNS lookups so network operators and ISPs cannot easily see which websites you request, improving privacy and reliability.

What SafeSurf Does

  • Encrypts DNS queries using DoH (DNS over HTTPS).
  • Prevents passive observers from seeing your domain lookups.
  • Helps access sites affected by DNS-level blocking (where applicable).
  • Designed to be lightweight — no system-wide VPN tunnel is created.

Why Choose SafeSurf

SafeSurf focuses specifically on DNS privacy — it's faster and uses less battery than a full VPN because it only secures DNS traffic (not the entire network stack).

Key Features

  • DNS-over-HTTPS (DoH) support
  • No activity or browsing logs
  • Simple one-tap activation
  • Small install size and minimal permissions

Limitations

SafeSurf protects DNS lookup privacy only. It does not route or encrypt all application traffic like a full VPN. If you need full traffic routing, consider a traditional VPN product.
