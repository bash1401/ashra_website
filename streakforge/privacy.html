Privacy Policy

How StreakForge handles your information

Last updated: July 10, 2026

1. Introduction

StreakForge (“the App”) is developed by BashTech (package com.bashtect.streakforge). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App processes, how it is used, and your choices.

2. Data stored on your device

Your habit and progress data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Tasks, counters, streaks, completions, and skip rules
  • Journal entries, challenges, badges, XP, and settings
  • Reminders and widget display data
  • Backups and CSV exports you create

This data is not uploaded to BashTech servers. We do not operate user accounts or cloud sync for habit tracking.

3. Habit catalog (Firebase)

The App may download a read-only catalog of predefined habit templates from Google Firebase (Firestore) so you can search and select habits during setup. This request fetches public template metadata only (names, icons, goals, categories).

  • No login is required
  • Your personal habit data is not sent to Firebase
  • Downloaded templates are cached on your device for offline browsing

Firebase is operated by Google. See Firebase Privacy and Google Privacy Policy.

4. Permissions

  • Notifications & alarms — optional local reminders
  • Storage / file access — backup, restore, and CSV import/export you initiate
  • Internet — habit catalog download only

5. Third-party services

  • Google Firebase / Firestore — public habit template catalog (read-only)
  • Google Play — app distribution, updates, and in-app subscriptions
  • Google AdMob — optional ads for free users (see Google’s advertising policies)

6. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your choices

  • Delete all app data anytime in Settings → Reset App Data
  • Disable notifications in app or system settings
  • Use the app without selecting catalog habits
