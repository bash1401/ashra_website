Features, specialties & how StreakForge works

Last updated: July 10, 2026 · Package com.bashtect.streakforge

What makes StreakForge special

  • Offline-first life scoreboard — habit data lives on your device; no account required.
  • Combined activity heatmap — GitHub-style calendar across all habits, not one task at a time.
  • Per-habit reminders — multiple daily times, notification actions (+1 / Snooze / Skip).
  • Life Dashboard — daily score, life areas, lifetime totals, and personal records.
  • Regional habit catalog — searchable templates (Firebase) with local customization.
  • Streak recovery — restore a missed day (Premium free weekly restores, or rewarded ad).

Core features

  • Yes/No & Counter habits — daily or weekly targets, units, colors, icons.
  • Streak engine — current/longest streak, skip weekdays, planned skips.
  • Home screen — list/grid views, quick complete / +1, wallpaper accents.
  • Task detail — charts, 90-day heatmap, multiple reminders (toggle / edit / delete).
  • Onboarding setup — pick habits, set reminder times, request notification permission.
  • Widgets — home widget synced with your top habits.
  • Backup & export — database backup/restore and CSV export from Settings.
  • Achievements — badges, XP, and level names as you stay consistent.

Reminders & notifications

  • Local notifications only (no cloud push for habit data).
  • Android 13+ asks for notification permission on the setup reminders step.
  • Notification actions: +1 (complete/increment), Snooze (10 minutes), Skip (mark skipped today).
  • On MIUI/Samsung, allow notifications and disable battery restrictions for reliable alarms.

Life Dashboard

  • Today’s score and overall life score across life areas.
  • Per-habit status for today (done / skipped / progress).
  • 90-day combined activity heatmap for all habits.
  • Lifetime totals and personal records.

Premium

  • Unlimited habits (free limit: 3).
  • No launch ads.
  • Weekly free streak restores, then optional rewarded ad.
  • Share progress with app promotion link.
  • Monthly and yearly plans — prices come from Google Play for your country/currency.

Privacy snapshot

Habit completions, streaks, and journals stay on-device. Firebase is used only for a read-only public habit catalog. Ads and Play Billing may process device/purchase data under Google’s policies. Full details: Privacy Policy.
