About StreakForge

Your personal offline life scoreboard

What is StreakForge?

StreakForge helps you build consistent habits with streak tracking, counters, life-area dashboards, monthly challenges, badges, widgets, and local reminders — without requiring an account.

Built by BashTech

StreakForge is part of the BashTech family of Android apps focused on privacy, productivity, and everyday utility.

View all BashTech apps · Google Play developer page

Key features

  • Yes/No and counter habits with daily or weekly targets
  • Skip days, recovery tokens, and streak engine
  • Life Dashboard with heatmaps and personal records
  • Searchable habit catalog with local customization
  • Backup, restore, and CSV export
  • Home screen widgets and notification quick actions
