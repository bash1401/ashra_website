Frequently asked questions

Reminders not firing?

Check that notifications and exact alarms are allowed for 21 Day Habit. Disable battery restrictions / autostart on some Android devices. Open Settings → Notification Diagnostics inside the app.

How do I restore Premium?

Go to Settings → Account → Restore Purchases while signed into the same Google Play account used to subscribe.

Can I create more than one habit?

Free users can have one active habit. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited habits.

Where is my data stored?

On your device. Use Premium export or Settings → Data → Backup/Export to save a copy.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Google Play → Subscriptions and cancel 21 Day Habit Premium.
