Privacy Policy

How 21 Day Habit handles your information

Last updated: July 5, 2026

1. Introduction

21 Day Habit (“the App”) is developed by BashTech. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App processes, how it is used, and your choices. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

2. Information stored on your device

The App stores habit data locally on your device, including:

  • Habit names, schedules, repeat days, focus duration, and notes
  • Completion history, streaks, badges, and statistics
  • Premium status and app preferences

This data is stored in on-device local databases and is not uploaded to BashTech servers by default.

3. Permissions

The App may request:

  • Notifications & exact alarms — window reminders and optional ringing alarm on your chosen days and times
  • Storage access — only if you pick a custom alarm audio file
  • Wake lock — to keep the screen on during focus timer sessions
  • Internet — for ads (free users), in-app purchases, and optional app-review prompts

You can revoke notification permissions in your device settings at any time.

4. Advertising (free users)

Free users may see ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data according to Google’s Privacy Policy. Premium subscribers do not see ads.

5. In-app purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive your full payment card details. The app verifies subscription status with Google Play when you open it; expired subscriptions revert to the free plan. Google handles payment information according to its own privacy policy.

6. Data export & import

Premium users can export habit data as a JSON file and share or back it up. Import restores data locally on your device. You control where exported files are stored or shared.

7. Third-party services

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) — advertising
  • Google Play Billing — subscriptions
  • Google Play In-App Review — optional rating prompts

8. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
