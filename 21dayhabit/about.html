About 21 Day Habit

One habit. One focus window. Twenty-one days to make it stick.

21 Day Habit is a productivity app by BashTech that helps you build consistency through a simple daily ritual: pick one habit, show up during your scheduled window, complete a focus session, and grow your streak.

What makes it different

  • Strict daily window — complete your habit within a focused time window or your streak resets (with optional streak freezes).
  • 21-day cycles — earn badges and continue to longer goals (42 or 66 days).
  • Reliable reminders — weekday-aware alarms with heads-up and action notifications.
  • Local-first privacy — your habit data stays on your device unless you choose to export it.

Premium
