How 21 Day Habit works — features, windows, alarms & premium

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Overview

21 Day Habit helps you build one habit at a time through daily focus sessions, strict completion windows, streak tracking, and smart reminders. Data is stored locally on your device. Free users get one active habit; Premium unlocks unlimited habits, removes ads, and adds backup/export.

Creating a habit

  • Name & schedule — pick habit name, daily time, duration, and repeat days (weekdays).
  • Goal length — 21, 42, or 66-day cycles with milestone badges.
  • Completion window — you can complete your habit from 30 minutes before scheduled time until 30 minutes after the session ends.
  • Ringing alarm — optional loud alarm (on by default). Choose how many minutes before habit time it rings: 0, 5, 10, 15, or 30 min.
  • Notifications — always sent at window start (“window started”) and window end (“window ended”), even if ringing alarm is off.

Daily flow

  1. Receive window-start notification when your completion window opens.
  2. Optionally hear a ringing alarm X minutes before habit time (if enabled).
  3. Open the app and start the focus timer for your habit duration.
  4. Complete the session to mark the day done and grow your streak.
  5. If you miss the window, your streak resets unless a Streak Freeze protects you.

Completion window & late finish

  • Example: habit at 7:00 AM, 20 min duration → window is 6:30 AM – 7:50 AM.
  • After the window closes, free users can still finish by watching a rewarded video.
  • Premium users get 3 late passes per week without watching ads.

Alarms & sounds

  • Ringing alarm — full-screen native alarm with Stop and Snooze (Android).
  • Alarm sound — pick from your phone’s system alarm tones, choose a custom audio file, or use device default (Settings → Alarm).
  • Snooze — configurable snooze duration, max snoozes, and auto-stop minutes.
  • Notifications — use your device’s default notification sound (no separate reminder tone picker).

Streaks, freezes & badges

  • Streak — consecutive days completed inside the window.
  • Streak Freeze — protects one missed day. Free: earn via rewarded ad. Premium: claim instantly.
  • Badges — earned at 21-day milestones; share progress cards.
  • History & stats — heatmap, completion rate, weekly patterns.

Subscriptions

  • Monthly and yearly plans via Google Play Billing.
  • Subscriptions renew automatically until cancelled in Google Play → Subscriptions.
  • When a subscription expires, the app checks with Google Play on open and reverts to the free plan.
  • Use Settings → Restore Purchases on a new device.

Settings reference

  • Notifications — master toggle; re-schedules all habit reminders.
  • Habit settings — ringing alarm on/off, alarm lead time per habit.
  • Alarm — tone, snooze, max snoozes, auto-stop.
  • Data — backup, export, import (Premium).
  • Notification diagnostics — troubleshoot missed reminders.

Privacy & data

All habit data is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. Nothing is uploaded to BashTech servers by default. Premium export creates a JSON file you control. See our Privacy Policy for AdMob, billing, and permissions.

Troubleshooting

  • Reminders not firing? Allow notifications and exact alarms; disable battery optimization / enable autostart (MIUI, etc.).
  • Alarm screen not showing? Grant display pop-ups and autostart permissions on some Android skins.
  • Premium not restoring? Same Google Play account → Settings → Restore Purchases.
