BashTech ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the GPA Calculator mobile application ("App").

2.1 Academic Data

We collect and store the following academic information:

  • Semester Information: Semester names, dates, and academic periods

  • Custom Grading Systems: User-created grading systems and templates

2.2 Account Information

If you choose to sign in with Google:

  • Email Address: For account identification
  • Display Name: Your Google account display name
  • Profile Photo: Your Google account profile picture
  • User ID: Unique identifier from Google

2.3 Device Information

We may collect:

  • Device Type: Android or iOS
  • App Version: Current version of the App
  • Operating System: Version and type
  • Device Identifiers: For analytics and crash reporting

2.4 Usage Data

We collect anonymized usage information:

  • Feature Usage: Which features are used most frequently
  • App Performance: Crash reports and performance metrics
  • Session Duration: How long you use the App
  • Error Logs: Technical issues for debugging

  • GPA Calculation: Process your grades to calculate GPA

3.2 Service Improvement
