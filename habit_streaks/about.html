Our Story

2023 - The Inspiration

Habit Streaks was born from the realization that building lasting habits requires more than willpower - it needs the right tools and motivation.

2024 - Development & Testing

Months of development focused on creating a privacy-first, user-friendly habit tracking solution that actually helps people succeed.

2024 - Launch

Habit Streaks launches on Google Play Store, bringing effective habit tracking to users worldwide with a focus on privacy and simplicity.
