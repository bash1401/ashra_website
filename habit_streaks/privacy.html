Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Learn how Habit Streaks protects your personal data.

Last updated: December 2024

About Habit Streaks

Habit Streaks is a habit tracking application developed by BashTech that helps you build and maintain positive habits through streak tracking and motivational features. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Habit Streaks, developed by BashTech. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Habit Streaks, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Habit Streaks is designed with privacy-first principles. Here's what we collect and how:

Device Information

Basic device information such as device model, operating system version, and app version for technical support and app optimization.

Usage Analytics

Anonymous usage statistics to improve app functionality and user experience. No personal data is collected.

Important: All habit data, streaks, and personal information are stored locally on your device. We do not access or transmit your personal habit data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide habit tracking and streak monitoring functionality
  • Generate progress statistics and insights
  • Send personalized reminders and notifications
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and optimize app functionality
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Analyze anonymous usage patterns to enhance features

4. Data Security

We implement several security measures to protect your data:

Local Storage

All habit data, streaks, and personal information are stored locally on your device using secure local storage.

No Cloud Storage

We do not store your habit data on external servers or cloud platforms.

Data Protection

All sensitive data is protected using industry-standard security measures.

Access Control

Only you have access to your habit data through the app's secure interface.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. However, we may disclose your information in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or to comply with legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With your explicit consent
Your Control: You have complete control over your data. You can delete all app data at any time through your device settings.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Habit Streaks does not use cookies or tracking technologies. Since all data is processed locally on your device, we do not need to track your online activity.

7. Children's Privacy

Habit Streaks is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, no international data transfers occur. Your data remains within your device's jurisdiction.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
